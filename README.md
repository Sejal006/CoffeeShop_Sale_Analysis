# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

The **Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard** is a data visualization project designed to analyze sales performance of a coffee shop across multiple categories, cities, and time periods.
The dashboard provides insights into **revenue, profit, transaction trends, and product performance**, helping businesses make data-driven decisions.

This dashboard was created to demonstrate **business intelligence and data analysis skills using dashboard visualization tools**.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze **overall sales performance**
* Identify **top-performing product categories**
* Monitor **monthly revenue and profit trends**
* Compare **yearly revenue performance**
* Analyze **sales distribution across cities**
* Track **weekday transaction patterns**

---

## 📈 Dashboard KPIs

The dashboard provides key performance indicators:

| KPI                 | Value   |

These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of overall business performance.

## 📊 Dashboard Visualizations

### 1️⃣ Category Transaction Analysis

Shows the number of transactions across product categories such as:

* Desserts
* Sandwiches
* Tea Beverages
* Coffee Beverages
* Specialty Drinks
* Snacks
* Cold Drinks

**Insight:** Desserts and Sandwiches have the highest transaction count.

---

### 2️⃣ Weekday Transaction Trend

Displays transaction count from **Sunday to Saturday**.

**Insight:**
Friday shows the highest transaction volume, indicating peak customer activity.

---

### 3️⃣ Yearly Revenue Distribution

A donut chart comparing revenue contributions across:

* 2021
* 2022
* 2023

**Insight:** Revenue distribution is nearly equal across all years (~33%).

---

### 4️⃣ Revenue by Category

Displays revenue generated by each product category.

**Insight:** Desserts generate the highest revenue.

---

### 5️⃣ Monthly Transaction Trend

Compares monthly transactions across **three years**.

**Insight:**
Sales show steady growth with seasonal fluctuations.

---

### 6️⃣ Revenue by City

Shows revenue contribution from cities including:

* Austin
* Chicago
* Los Angeles
* New York
* Seattle

**Insight:** Chicago and Los Angeles generate the highest revenue.

---

### 7️⃣ Monthly Revenue vs Profit

Compares **monthly revenue and profit performance**.

**Insight:** Profit follows a similar trend to revenue, indicating consistent margins.
